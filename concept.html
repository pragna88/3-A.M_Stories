What is a 3:AM story?

‘We do not talk, we burgeon one another with facts and theories gleaned from cursory readings of newspapers, magazines and digests.’

- Henry Miller

Henry Miller wrote it in 1945, it's still the same. We talk ,but we rarely communicate. We figured out there is one golden time on the clock when we really communicate. It's 3:AM. You must have had a fun night with friends or family. We talk till 2 but we start sharing what really matters at 3:AM. This is the place where you can share your 3:AM stories at any time. We are here to listen. It can be type of story. Funny.Scary.Feel good. Embarrassing. Sad. Relationship. Crime. Anything!! We want to here it. 3:AM Story is a story that connects us all. When you share,you feel better. All the stories written on this site are by anonymous people.Many people emailed us saying it feels good when they write what they want to express without getting judged.These stories won't be conventional attractive one, but needed to get heard. We want to hear it,with complete anonymity to our 3AM stories sharers.We wouldn't comment but respond.This is the place you want to be. You can make your own category.Write your own story. This is your time. Go to categories, write. We are waiting.
